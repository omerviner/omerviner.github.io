A board and cubes game.
Generating a story based on Peter and the Wolf.
Decoration cubes
Decision cubes
Board & flow information
Placing a cube closes either one or two circuits,
depending on the cube's orientation.
The number of closed circuits informs us of the player's choice.
The next part of the story is determined
using the cube's orientation and then revealed.

The story is built by a decision tree.
